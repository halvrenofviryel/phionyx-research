# Phionyx Defensive Patent Pledge

**Date:** 2026-04-04
**Pledgor:** Phionyx Project (Ali Toygar Abak, Founder)

## Pledge

All patents and patent applications owned by or filed on behalf of the Phionyx
project are held for **defensive purposes only**.

We irrevocably pledge that these patents will **not** be used to initiate patent
infringement claims against:

1. **Open-source projects** — any software distributed under an OSI-approved license
2. **Academic research** — any work conducted for educational or research purposes
3. **Non-commercial use** — any use that does not generate direct commercial revenue

## Scope

This pledge applies to:
- All current patent applications filed by the Phionyx project
- All future patent applications filed by the Phionyx project
- All continuations, divisionals, and foreign counterparts of the above

## Defensive Use

Patents may only be asserted in response to a patent infringement claim first
brought against the Phionyx project, its contributors, or its users.

## Irrevocability

This pledge is **irrevocable**. It cannot be withdrawn, modified, or narrowed
by the pledgor or any successor in interest, except to expand its protections.
